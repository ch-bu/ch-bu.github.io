Design theories are intended to provide direkt guidance to practioners about what methods to use to attain different goals, whereas descriptive theories attempt to provide a deeper understanding of effects that result from phenomena. Therefore, descriptive theories are also useful to practitioners, because they provide an understanding of why a design theory works and because they help practioners to generate their own design theories fro those many situations for which no adequate ones exist. The major concer for people developing and testing descriptive theories is validity, whereas for design theories, it is preferability (i.e. does this method attain your goals for your situation better than any other know method?). (Reigeluth & Carr-Chellman, 2009, S. 8)

Goal-based scenarios

Because they are products of our current educational system, most adults are accustomed to learning theories by lecture and demonstrating that knowledge by multiple-choice tests. But it is a bad idea to delude ourselves that this process constitutes actual learning. The real learning that takes place in business takes place on the job. Elaboration Theory

The primary goal of this theory is to help select and sequence content in a way that will optimize attainment of learning goals. It is intended for medium to complex kinds of cognitive and psychomotor learning, but does not currently deal with content that is primarily in the affective domain. (Reigeluth & Carr-Chellman, 2009, S. 8)

Instructional Design Modelle und E-Learning

Die Aufgabe eines Instructional-Designers besteht darin, das richtige Instructional Design Modell für ein bestimmtes Lehrziel auszusuchen.

Aufgabe

Überlegt euch in eurer Kleingruppe, welches Instructional Design Modell am ehesten den letzten Online Lernumgebungen unterliegt. Bennent Gründe, weshalb Ihr euch für dieses Modell entschieden habt. Gibt es zudem Elemente anderer Instructional Design Modelle, die eurer Lernumgebung unterliegen (15 min).

Aufgabe

Stellt euch in eurer Kleingruppe euer momentanes Produkt vor. Diskutiert, was ein gutes Lernziel dieser Lernumgebung sein könnte und welches Instructional Design Modell ihr für euer Produkt einsetzen könntet (40 min).
